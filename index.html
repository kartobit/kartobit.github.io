Mission

KartoBit is a open research network for Geographic Information Science(GIS) with researchers from China, Germany and Norway. The talent network focuses on developing computational methods to enable discovery, representation and applicaiton of geospatial knowledge for both human- and machine-centered applications, e.g., smart city, robotic autonomy. Dr. Jian Yang lead the collective effort since 2016, after finishing his doctoral study at Lehrstuhl für Kartographie, Technische Universtität München.


R&D Clusters

  • GeoKDD: Knowledge Discovery from Spatio-Temporal Datasets
  • CogAgent: Cognitive Computing for Intelligent Agents
  • CrowdNav: Crowdsourced Navigation for Quality-Critical Applicaitions
